Elsweyr Fondue

Description

Elsweyr Fondue a delicious and hearty dish popular in Skyrim. Nothing quite beats gathering with friends and family around the fondue pot, sharing the simple joy of warm cheese and fresh bread. Elsweyr fondue is unique in that it utilises three ingredients: ale, moon sugar, and an Eidar cheese wheel. As such, the cheesy flavour is made more complex with the use of blue Eidar cheese, as well as the warm bitterness of ale and a hint of sweetness from the moon sugar.

Ingredients:

  • Fondue pot and skewers
  • 1 bottle of ale, left overnight to go flat
  • 100g gruyere cheese, grated
  • 100g emmental cheese, grated
  • 100g soft blue cheese, diced
  • Brown sugar, to taste
  • Fresh bread, cut into cubes

Steps:

  1. Set up your fondue pot.
  2. Add the ale and sugar, and stir until sugar has melted.
  3. Add the grated cheese bit by bit, stirring to ensure it melts smoothly.
  4. Once the emmental and gruyere are melted, add the blue cheese pieces and stir until the mixture is completely smooth.
  5. Pass a bowl of bread cubes around the table and enjoy!
